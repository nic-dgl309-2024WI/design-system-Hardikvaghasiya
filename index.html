Welcome to Outback Nursery & Garden Centre

Outback Nursery & Garden Centre, nestled on a 6-acre plot on Vancouver Island, has grown into a significant garden center since its inception in 1998-1999. Starting with farm animal care and hedging sales, it now specializes in Japanese Maples, Conifers, and more, serving both wholesale and public garden enthusiasts with a wide array of plants and garden accessories​
